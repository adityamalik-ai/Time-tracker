Consent & transparency

You have full visibility into what PW.live Tracker collects about you.

  • Tracking only activates when you press Start and you're logged in to a @pw.live account
  • Only domain names are recorded — never page titles, content, or keystrokes
  • Incognito tabs and personal browser profiles are completely excluded
  • You can request full deletion of your data at any time
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
