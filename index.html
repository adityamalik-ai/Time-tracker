📌 Tab Logger Bookmarklet
A bookmarklet is a bookmark that runs code. Drag the button below to your Chrome bookmarks bar. Then, while on any browser tab (Gmail, WhatsApp, etc.), just click it once — it instantly sends that tab's domain to your PW Tracker.
⬇ DRAG THIS TO YOUR BOOKMARKS BAR ⬇
📌 Log This Tab → PW Tracker
How it works:
  1. Drag the button above to your Chrome bookmarks bar
  2. Open any tab (Gmail, WhatsApp, GitHub, etc.)
  3. Click "Log This Tab → PW Tracker" in your bookmarks bar
  4. The domain is instantly sent to your tracker tab ✓
Why a bookmarklet? Browsers block webpages from reading other tabs' URLs for privacy reasons. A bookmarklet runs inside the tab you click it on — so it can read that tab's URL and broadcast it to the tracker. No extension install needed.
